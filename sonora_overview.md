## Executive Summary

Sonora is an AI-native customer intelligence platform designed for post-sales teams. The company helps businesses turn unstructured customer data into actionable insights to reduce churn, accelerate upsell opportunities & improve product feedback cycles.

---

## Product Overview

### Core Value Proposition
Sonora brings structure to messy, unstructured customer data by integrating multiple data sources & applying AI/ML models to surface actionable signals for customer success teams.

### Key Features
- **Unified Customer Data Platform** : Aggregates data from product usage, support cases, meeting notes & email
- **AI Copilot** : Helps teams search, explore & take action on customer signals
- **Proactive Risk Detection** : Surfaces churn risk signals automatically
- **NDR (Net Dollar Retention) Optimization** : Identifies upsell & expansion opportunities
- **Product Feedback Intelligence** : Accelerates product feedback cycles by analyzing customer sentiment & feature requests

### Data Sources
- Product usage metrics
- Support case history
- Meeting notes & transcriptions
- Email communications
- Public web sources (for enrichment)

### Technology Stack
Sonora uses tailored ML & AI models specifically designed to extract signals from unstructured data. Their approach focuses on delivering high-quality insights rather than generic AI outputs.

Sonora operates in the customer intelligence & CS operations space, likely competing with or complementing :
- Traditional customer success platforms (Gainsight, Totango, ChurnZero)

**Key Differentiator** : AI-native approach to unstructured data extraction & signal detection, rather than retrofitting AI onto legacy CS platforms.
