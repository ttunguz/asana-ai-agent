## Product & Technology ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê

### Core Value Proposition
"Construction AI workflows you can trust with a precise drawing & spec paper trail, from precon to closeout"

### Key Features

1. **Connected Documents**
   - Automatically links every detail (door tags, equipment specs, finish schedules) to corresponding drawings & specifications
   - Creates "construction knowledge graph"
   - Enables instant traceability

2. **AI Assistant ("Ask Marvin")**
   - Natural language queries (e.g., "Show me all fire-rated doors on level 3")
   - Answers traceable to exact drawing details or spec section references
   - Eliminates manual document hunting

3. **Constructability Review**
   - Flags design conflicts before field problems occur
   - Reduces manual review from weeks to instant analysis
   - Example conflicts: dimension mismatches, missing specs, incompatible materials

4. **Automated Submittal Review**
   - First-pass product data review as submittals arrive from trade partners
   - Validates against specifications automatically
   - Accelerates approval workflows

### Technical Differentiation

**Multimodal AI Core:**
- Reads both visual (drawings) & text (specifications) simultaneously
- Likely leverages Bourdev's computer vision expertise from Facebook AI
- Goes beyond simple OCR or text parsing

**Audit Trail Architecture:**
- Every AI answer links to source document & page
- Critical for construction industry (liability, disputes, compliance)
- Builds trust in AI recommendations

**Integration Strategy:**
- Works with Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC) & Procore
- Not rip-and-replace - augments existing workflows
- Lowers adoption friction vs. standalone platform

### Product Maturity Questions
- How accurate is conflict detection? (False positive rate?)
- What's the human review requirement?
- How much training data required per project?
- What's the onboarding complexity?

**Assessment:** Product is differentiated & addresses real pain point. Multimodal AI + construction-specific = technical moat potential. Need to validate accuracy & adoption friction.
